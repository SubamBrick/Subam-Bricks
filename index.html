About Subam Bricks

Subam Bricks has been a trusted name in Theni for over 20 years. We specialize in high-quality, durable bricks crafted with precision and passion.

Founded by Mr. Pandian K and co-led by Mr. Sabarinathan P, our mission is to build the future, one brick at a time — with strength, sustainability, and trust.

Our Manufacturing Process

At Subam Group of Companies, our bricks are produced using advanced automated chamber wire-cut machinery to ensure uniformity, strength, and durability. The raw clay is finely filtered and processed through modern equipment, where it is precisely cut into consistent sizes using high-precision molds. Each brick is stamped with our brand to ensure quality. The grooved surface ensures strong bonding, making it perfect for all types of construction.
